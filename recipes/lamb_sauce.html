Learn how to make Gordon Ramsey's infamous Lamb Sauce!

Gordon Ramsay's Lamb Sauce refers to a series of shitpost style images referencing a 2006 episode of Hell's Kitchen in which Chef Gordon Ramsay berates a kitchen full of chefs, demanding to know where the lamb sauce is for a dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dry red wine, or water
  • 1 cup chicken stock, or reduced-sodium canned broth
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Garlic-Roasted Leg of Lamb

Directions

  1. Step 1

    2. Pour red wine into the roasting pan in which the Lamb was cooked.

  2. Step 2

    3. Place over medium heat, and scrape up browned bits from bottom of pan.

  3. Step 3

    4. Add stock and salt; bring to a simmer. Cook, stirring, until liquid has reduced to 1 1/2 cups,

    about 10 minutes. Skim fat from surface, as needed.

  4. Step 4

    5. Strain sauce into a gravy boat, and serve hot with sliced lamb.
